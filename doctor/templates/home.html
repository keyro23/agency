    Down to business!

    Are you ready to get down to business? We've got pricing options that'll knock your socks off! We can create a customized quotation that's tailor-made just for you, or if you prefer, we'll share our handy dandy rate card. Whatever your preference, we're all about keeping things transparent and straightfoward. So let's crunch some numbers and find the perfect solution for you!

    Step 5. Contract/Agreement

    Let's Agree

    Once we've discussed the details of the project and agreed on the scope of work, we'll put together a customized service agreement. This agreement outlines all the terms and conditions of our partnership and is shared with the prospective client for review. If everything lokoks good and the client agrees to the terms, we'll move foward and sign the contract, allowing the project to commence. We take our commitments seriously and believe in creating a solid foundation of trust and transparency with our clients.

    Step 6. Invoicing

    Required!

    After the client has agreed to move foward with the project and signed the service agreement, we'll proceed to invoiving. We require a one-month upfront to be paid before we begin the work. This allows us to allocate the necessary resources and start the project off on the right foot. Our payment process is straightfoward and designed to provide peace of mind to our clients, knowing that their project is in good hands.

    At this stage, our team puts their heads together to craft the perfect social media strategy of our clients. We'll create a one month content calender that's specifically tailored to their unique goals and objectives. Before we get started, we'll share the calender with the client for any necessary changes or approval.     We Dive to Implementation
